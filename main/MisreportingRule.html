In addition to the $2 of fixed compensation you will receive, you can earn an extra bonus based on the points you report, with a maximum bonus of $2.

If you report 16 points or less, you will receive no bonus. For each score above 16, you will earn an extra $0.50.

For example:

  • You report 16 points, bonus= $0.50 * (16 - 16) = $0
  • You report 19 points, bonus= $0.50 * (19 - 16) = $1.5

On the next page, you will be asked to report your score. Please note that the bonus payment is based solely on the score you report, not your actual accuracy. You are free to report any score.

However, there is a possibility that your reported score may be cross-checked by others. If this happens, and your reported number is found to be inaccurate, there is a probability that you will lose all your bonus, yet the fixed payment of $2 will remain unaffected.
